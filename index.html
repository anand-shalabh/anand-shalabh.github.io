Research interests

I am a condensed matter theorist working at the interface of soft condensed matter physics, materials science, and biology. I develop and apply the tools of statistical and computational physics to study physical and biological systems, including biological motors, synthetic molecular machines, colloids, thermoelectric machines, biological fluids, and active matter. I follow an interdisciplinary approach working closely with physicists, chemists, chemical engineers, materials scientists, and specialists in machine learning.
